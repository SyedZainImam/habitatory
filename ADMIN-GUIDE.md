# Habitatory — Admin Guide (How to Update the Website)

## Opening the Admin Panel

1. Open your browser
2. Go to: **your-website-url/studio** (e.g., `http://localhost:3000/studio`)
3. Log in with your Sanity account

## What You Can Change

### Site Settings (Phone, Email, Address, Tagline)

1. Click **"Site Settings"** in the left sidebar
2. Edit any field:
   - **Company Name** — shown in the hero section
   - **Tagline** — shown below the company name on the homepage
   - **Contact Email** — shown in the footer
   - **Phone Number** — shown in the footer
   - **Address** — shown in the footer
   - **Social Media Links** — Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn URLs
   - **About Section Text** — the paragraph under "Our Process"
3. Click the green **"Publish"** button at the bottom
4. Refresh your website to see the changes

### Adding/Editing Events

1. Click **"Events"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **"+"** button to add a new event
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — name of the event
   - **Slug** — click "Generate" (this creates the URL)
   - **Category** — pick Corporate, Wedding, or Custom
   - **Event Date** — when the event happened
   - **Cover Image** — the main photo (drag and drop or click to upload)
   - **Gallery Images** — additional photos
   - **Description** — a short description
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Adding/Editing Services

1. Click **"Services"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"+"** to add a new service
3. Fill in the title and description
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Adding Testimonials

1. Click **"Testimonials"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"+"** to add a new testimonial
3. Fill in:
   - **Client Name** — who said it
   - **Quote** — what they said
   - **Event Reference** — (optional) link to one of your events
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Managing Hero Slides (Homepage Carousel)

1. Click **"Hero Slides"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"+"** to add a new slide
3. Upload an image and set the **Display Order** (1 = first, 2 = second, etc.)
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Viewing Contact Inquiries

1. Click **"Contact Inquiries"** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see messages from the contact form
3. Entries with a **dot (●)** before the name are unread
4. Open an inquiry and check the **"Read"** checkbox to mark it as read

## Important Notes

- Always click **"Publish"** after making changes — unpublished changes are drafts and won't show on the website
- Changes appear on the website after refreshing the page
- You can upload images by dragging and dropping them into the image fields
- To delete something, open it and click the **"..."** menu → **"Delete"**
